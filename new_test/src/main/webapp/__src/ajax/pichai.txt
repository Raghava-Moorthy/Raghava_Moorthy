<h1>Sundar Pichai</h1> 
<p>Pichai Sundararajan, better known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-American business executive.</p> 
<p>He is the chief executive officer of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google.</p> 
<p>Born in Madras, India, Pichai earned his degree from IIT Kharagpur in metallurgical engineering.</p>
